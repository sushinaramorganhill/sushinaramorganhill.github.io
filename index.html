Welcome to Sushi Nara

Authentic Japanese
Restaurant in the
Heart of Morgan Hill

Established in 2010, Sushi Nara has been serving our community for more than 10 years, We pride ourselves on offering the freshest, most delicious ingredients and seafood available in the market place. Our fresh fish comes to us from all over the globe as we only deliver the best quality to and for our customers.

    Catering

    Sushi for Every Occassion

    Whether you host a special gathering at home or bring lunch for your children’s school event, see how Sushi Nara catering can shine your time. We serve food with excellent presentations. We offer everything from our menu for your catering plates.

      [How To Order]
    • 1. Find dishes you want to order
    • 2. Call us and order in advance 24 hours prior to your event
      (Dinner time pick-up is available with a morning order)
    • 3. Let us know that you want them to be put on catering plates
    • 4. We will text you when your order is ready
    • 5. Enjoy!

    *We will charge $5 per tray.
